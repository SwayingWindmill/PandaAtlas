Chengdu Giant Pandas Xing Er and Mao Er Handover to Denmark Ceremony Held in Chengdu Research Base

The two giant pandas would be sent to Denmark from Chengdu on April 4 at 7:30. They will stay at the Copenhagen Zoo.

Introductions to the giant pandas from the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding to Denmark:

Xing Er: Pedigree No.: 900, sex: male, born on August 23, 2013, father: Yong Yong, mother: Xing Rong. He is lively and lovely, and loves to climb trees and take baths.

Mao Er: Pedigree No.: 919, nickname: Mao Sun, sex: female, born on July 26, 2014, father: Xiong Bang, mother: Fu Wa. She has a quiet personality and prefers being alone.
