Giant Panda FAQs

Visiting the Pandas at the Smithsonian's National Zoo

The giant pandas’ names are Bao Li [BOW-lee] and Qing Bao [ching-BOW].

Bao Li was born Aug. 4, 2021, at the China Conservation and Research Center for the Giant Panda (CCRCGP) in Sichuan, to father An An and mother Bao Bao. Bao Li came to the Zoo from the Shenshuping Base in Wolong.

Qing Bao was born Sept. 12, 2021, at CCRCGP, to father Qing Qing and mother Jia Mei. Qing Bao came to the Zoo from the Dujiangyan Base in Sichuan.

Both giant pandas live at the David M. Rubenstein and Family Giant Panda Habitat.
